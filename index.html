Steps

QR Scan

Scan the QR code on a JB Charging bank, which will take you to our app (or website).

Payment Details

After the QR scan, you'll be prompted to enter your payment details through our secure platform.

Confirm Rental

When you confirm your rental, your charger will pop out of the bank, when this happens, you are good to go.

Return Charger

Conclude your JuiceBox experience by returning the charger to any of our stations located throughout major U.S. cities. Return within 72 hours to avoid late fees.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to get an app? We have a user friendly app but you can also rent a charger without one.
How much does it cost to charge? It costs $1.00 to unlock a charger + $1.00 per hour of charging.
What if I lose or damage a charger? The cost of an unreturned charger is $45.00.
Where is the nearest charging bank? Our app has a map showing every JB Charging bank with about the location.
Am I liable for any damages if I host a JB Charging bank in my shop? Nope, all liability on us.
